Welcome!

Welcome to the website for Dave Borncamp!

Hello, I am currently a Senior Research and Instrument Analyst at Space Telescope Science Institute. I work on the Hubble Space Telescope's Advanced Camera for Surveys instrument team where my work focuses on instrument calibrations to keep it producing awesome science. This includes advanced studies of the detector's dark current, geometric distortion, and flat fields. To accomplish my work I use Python and IDL for exploratory data analysis and software development.

My Science

For my science, I mainly focus on characterizations of Pluto and Kuiper Belt Objects. This came out of my degree in Astronomy at the University of Colorado where I worked with Marc Buie at Southwest Research Institute to characterize pluto and discover KBO's for the New Horizons Mission to look at after it passes Pluto.
